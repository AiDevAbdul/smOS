🎯 Blue Rose Auto — Meta Ad Strategy Playbook

🔧

Mechanic / Repair

  • Hook: Lead with a pain point — "Is your check engine light on?" or "Tired of overpriced dealership repairs?"
  • Format: Short video (before/after inspection) performs best
  • CTA: "Book Now" or "Get a Free Estimate"
  • Offer: Free multi-point inspection or $X off first service drives clicks
  • Angle: Local trust — show your team, shop, real reviews
🚗

Collision / Body Shop

  • Hook: "We work directly with all insurance companies"
  • Format: Before/after carousel — dramatic transformations win
  • CTA: "Free Estimate" is the #1 CTA in this category
  • Offer: Free rental car / free tow partnership adds perceived value
  • Angle: Insurance claim expertise removes customer fear
✨

Auto Detailing

  • Hook: Transformation content — dirty vs. clean is highly shareable
  • Format: Video reels get the most organic boost; use for ads too
  • CTA: "Book Today" + urgency ("Only 3 slots left this week")
  • Offer: Package bundles (interior + exterior) with a discount perform well
  • Angle: Mobile detailing angle ("We come to you") is a strong differentiator
🎨

Vehicle Wraps

  • Hook: "Turn your vehicle into a moving billboard"
  • Format: Portfolio carousel — show your best work
  • CTA: "Get a Free Quote" — high-consideration purchase
  • Offer: Lead with portfolio + social proof, not discounts
  • Angle: Target local business owners for commercial wraps (fleet)
🪟

Window Tinting

  • Hook: "Beat the heat. Protect your interior."
  • Format: Static image — clear before/after with shade comparison
  • CTA: "Book Now" with same-day availability
  • Offer: Seasonal promotions (summer heat) drive urgency
  • Angle: UV protection + privacy + energy savings (multiple pain points)
💎

Ceramic Coating

  • Hook: "Your car's paint deserves better than wax"
  • Format: High-quality video showing water beading / gloss
  • CTA: "Get a Free Consultation" — high-ticket, educate first
  • Offer: Limited-time pricing or bundle with PPF
  • Angle: Long-term value ("Protects for 5+ years") justifies price
🛡️

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

  • Hook: "Protect your investment from day one"
  • Format: Side-by-side comparison video or scratch demo
  • CTA: "Get a Free Quote" — PPF is a premium, researched purchase
  • Offer: Bundle PPF + ceramic coating for package pricing
  • Angle: New car owners are the #1 target — catch them early
🚀

Blue Rose Auto — Where to Start

  • Step 1: Launch with detailing + mechanic ads — highest ad volume, most tested playbooks
  • Step 2: Before/after video content is the #1 winning format across all categories
  • Step 3: Run a lead-gen ad with "Free Estimate / Free Inspection" offer
  • Step 4: Target: Springfield/Eugene OR, 25–55, vehicle owners, homeowners
  • Step 5: Bundle services (e.g. tint + ceramic + PPF) for higher-ticket packages
  • Budget: Start at $15–25/day per service category, scale what converts
