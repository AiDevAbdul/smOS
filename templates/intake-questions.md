# Intake Q&A Script

The `/intake` skill walks through these 9 groups conversationally. Ask one question at a time; parse the answer; ask the next. If an answer is ambiguous, ask one clarifying follow-up before moving on.

---

## 1. Business Basics

- What's the business name, and what product or service does it sell? (one paragraph max)
- What's the price range? Low to high.
- Business model: DTC, B2B, lead-gen, e-commerce, service-based, subscription?
- In one sentence — what makes this offer different from competitors?

## 2. Target Audience

- Primary age range? (e.g., 25–45)
- Gender skew? (all, mostly male, mostly female)
- Geographic targeting? (cities, regions, countries)
- Top 3 pain points the product solves?

## 3. Brand Voice

- Tone: professional, casual, educational, bold, friendly, urgent?
- Are there words or phrases that must NEVER appear in ads? (e.g., "cheap", "guaranteed", competitor names)
- Preferred CTA style: direct action ("Buy now"), soft pull ("Learn more"), curiosity ("See how")?

## 4. Accounts & Access

- Meta Ad Account ID (format `act_XXXXX`)
- Meta Pixel ID
- Facebook Page ID
- Instagram Business Account ID (if applicable)
- Business Manager ID (if applicable)

## 5. KPI Targets

- Target CPA (cost per acquisition or lead) in account currency?
- Target ROAS (return on ad spend), if e-commerce?
- Monthly budget range, low to high?
- What event counts as a "conversion" for this business? (purchase, lead form, call, signup)

## 6. History

- Have you run paid ads on Meta before? If yes, roughly how much total spend?
- What's worked in the past? (best campaign type, best creative angle)
- What's failed? (specific things that flopped, money wasted, audiences that didn't convert)

## 7. Competitive Context

- Top 3 direct competitors (names + websites if possible). We'll pull their ads from Meta Ad Library in Phase 3.

## 8. Assets

- What creative formats do you have ready? (product photos, lifestyle photos, video, UGC, testimonials)
- Brand guidelines doc URL, if any?
- Logo + brand color hex codes?

## 9. Approval Preferences

- Approval channel for sensitive actions: Discord, email, or both?
- Daily budget threshold above which Claude should NEVER auto-act? (default $500)
- Are there any actions you want auto-approved beyond the global defaults? (e.g., always auto-pause underperformers)
